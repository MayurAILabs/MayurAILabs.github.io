Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

Most tools on this site (Daily Savings Calculator) run entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server. Tools requiring sign-in (Expense Manager, Priority Manager) use Firebase Authentication and store your data in Cloud Firestore, tied to your account only.

The AI assistant

Messages you send to the AI assistant are processed by a Cloudflare Worker and forwarded to an AI provider to generate a reply. Conversation history is kept only in your browser's session storage and is cleared when you close the tab.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, run third-party ad trackers, or share your information with anyone beyond what's needed to operate the tool you're using.

Cookies & local storage

We use localStorage/sessionStorage for functional purposes only — theme preference, recent searches, and (for signed-in apps) your session. No third-party advertising cookies.
